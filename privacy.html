Privacy Policy

This application allows users to connect their own TikTok accounts through TikTok OAuth authorization.

We collect and store only the data necessary to provide the service, including basic account information, access tokens, uploaded video metadata, and performance analytics. All access is explicitly authorized by the user.

Data is used solely to enable content publishing and analytics features. We do not sell, scrape, or share user data with third parties.

Access tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the TikTok account. Upon disconnection, all associated credentials are deleted.

Users may request data deletion by contacting us.

This app complies with TikTok API policies and applicable data protection laws.
